Who we are

My name is Sebastian Krüger, your personal insurance broker for individuals who work abroad as freelancers. Have you ever wondered what it would be like to have a comprehensive and tailor-made insurance solution that truly suits you and your lifestyle? I am here to support you in achieving exactly that – in a straightforward and understandable manner. I offer you individual consultations to find the best solutions for your insurance and financial security together.

Independence and Expertise

As an independent insurance broker based in Berlin but accessible to you worldwide, I bring over 30 years of experience in insurance, finance, and event security. I specialize in assisting those who are gathering professional or personal experiences abroad – whether as a foreigner in Germany or Germans abroad. My service approach is focused on giving you the freedom to enjoy your life without worrying about your insurance.

Comprehensive Support Tailored to Your Needs

Rely on me to provide you with personal and always online-accessible support. I take the time to understand your individual needs and goals, so you can be sure that your financial security aspects are covered with tailored solutions. Personal contact is of utmost importance to me, and I strive to offer support wherever you may be in the world.

With Confidence in a Secure Future

Whether you are new to living abroad, already residing there for some time, or planning to move, with my help, you can focus on important things like your family, projects, and career goals. Together, we ensure you are well-protected and prepared for all eventualities. Trust me to be your reliable partner in insurance matters, enabling you to embark on a fulfilling life journey with confidence.
