PassportCard – Die Revolution der Krankenzahlung für Expats

Are you tired of paperwork and long reimbursement times? With PassportCard, I bring the first health insurance to Germany that pays your medical bills directly on the spot.

Why PassportCard for your stay in Germany?

  • Cashless payment: You receive a special card you can use worldwide (and of course in Germany) to pay for doctor visits and medication immediately.
  • No advance payments: Your private budget stays untouched – the insurer tops up the card in real time.
  • Worldwide protection: Ideal if you use Germany as your base but travel internationally often.
  • German service 24/7: An expert team in Hamburg is available for you around the clock.

Plans for every need:

Whether you want solid basic coverage or premium protection including preventive care and dental benefits – PassportCard offers flexible packages for your new chapter in Germany.
