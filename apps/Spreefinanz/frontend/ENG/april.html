International health insurance for expats in Germany

Welcome to Germany! Whether you come to us as a freelancer, employee or student – ​​the right health insurance is not only legally required, but also the key to your visa and your safety.

With APRIL International, we offer you a highly flexible, digital solution that is specifically tailored to the needs of international professionals in Germany.

Why choose APRIL International for your stay in Germany?

  • Visa guarantee: The tariffs are BaFin-certified and are fully recognized by the German immigration authorities for the issuance of residence permits.
  • 100% Digital & Mobile: Manage your insurance completely via app ("Easy Claim"). Simply submit medical bills by photo – reimbursement usually takes place within a few days.
  • Telemedicine 24/7: Free video consultations with doctors around the clock – in German, English and many other languages.
  • Flexibility: You decide on deductibles and additional modules such as dental or ophthalmological care.

Our rates for your start in Germany

Choose the appropriate insurance coverage based on your planned length of stay:
 

1. My Health International (Long-term & Permanent)

The ideal solution for expats, freelancers and digital nomads who want to stay in Germany for more than a year or indefinitely.
  • Benefit: Comprehensive health insurance including outpatient and inpatient treatment.
  • Special feature: Unlimited duration and worldwide protection (ideal if you want to be covered while traveling).
  • Risk assessment: Requires a brief health check for tailored protection.
